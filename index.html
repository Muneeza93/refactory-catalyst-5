Education is a basic human right that powers development and is directly responsible for eradicating poverty, increasing life expectancy, ensuring political stability and gender equality. For the case of individuals, education boosts employment opportunities, entrepreneurship, household income and poverty reduction. For Countries, education promotes sustainable economic growth through increase in revenue collection, strong government institutions free from corruption and spurs innovation.
